About Me

Aadhya Malhotra

Best known for her eponymous label, I'm much revered in the industry. My collection is an act of presenting India's rich craft encapsulated in global contours. My philosophy is about innovation with regards to the Indian style, while also keeping it extremely relevant to the modern world. For instance, the dhoti pant, commonly accredited to the brand, is now accepted as a modern high waist trouser worn with a dupatta or the Indian skirt. Teamed up with kurtas and capes, boots and shirts gives a perfect example of modern relevance.

I'm all about finding your own personal style and having the dream and vision to express them to the world around you.
